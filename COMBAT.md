## What we train
Start **Muay Thai as the anchor** (fastest real skill + conditioning, beginner-friendly) + **BJJ once a week** for grappling. Both included at BETA. That's well-rounded "combat training" without spreading thin.

## How many days — 3×/week
Tue + Thu (Muay Thai) + Sat (BJJ / open mat). Three is the sweet spot: enough to actually progress, sustainable alongside the business, the lifts, and recovery. Two is too slow to build reflexes; 4–5 is fighter territory and burns out beginners (and the bulk).

## Sign-up plan
1. **This week:** both book the **free trial** — a Muay Thai Fundamentals slot at the BETA location closest to us (NW vs Capitol Hill). Take it together.
2. **Lock membership** after the trial (ask about unlimited vs a class pack; mention you're doing Muay Thai + BJJ).
3. **Gear:** hand wraps + 16oz gloves + shin guards for Muay Thai; a gi for BJJ (gym often loans one to start). Mouthguard for both.
4. **Block it in the calendar** as recurring Tue/Thu/Sat — same times every week so it becomes automatic (subscribe via the dashboard's routine calendar).
5. **Accountability:** combat day counts as the daily "Train" non-negotiable — miss it and the $5 stake applies, same as a missed lift.
