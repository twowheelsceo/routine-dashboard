## The one idea that matters most
**Discipline = architecture, not willpower.** Build automatic behaviors in a fixed context, write if-then plans, track the streak (forgive one miss). The people with the best self-control win mainly on good automatic habits — not white-knuckle resistance (Galla & Duckworth 2015, N=2,274).
